
1. download boost 1.63.0

http://www.boost.org/

2. extract it to: C:\

path of it's content will then be:

C:\boost_1_63_0

3. open cmd/command line as adminb

4. enter into cmd: cd C:\boost_1_63_0

5. enter into cmd: bootstrap and wait some time

6. when that is done, enter the following into cmd and wait a long time: .\b2 --toolset=msvc-14.0 address-model=64 --with-asio

7. you are done.
